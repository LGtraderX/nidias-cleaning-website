Regular Cleaning vs Deep Cleaning for Large Homes in Bernardsville & Basking Ridge, NJ

Homeowners in Bernardsville and Basking Ridge often ask the same question: Should I schedule regular cleaning, or do I need deep cleaning? The answer depends on the size of your home, how it’s used, and how often it’s maintained.

What Is Regular Cleaning?

Regular cleaning focuses on weekly or biweekly maintenance. It’s ideal for homes that are already in good condition and need consistent upkeep.

  • Surface dusting
  • Vacuuming and mopping floors
  • Kitchen and bathroom surface cleaning
  • Trash removal

What Is Deep Cleaning?

Deep cleaning targets buildup and detail areas that aren’t covered in regular visits, which is common in larger homes.

  • Baseboards and trim
  • Door frames and switches
  • Detailed bathroom scrubbing
  • Kitchen buildup areas

Best Approach for Large Homes

Most homeowners start with a deep cleaning to reset the home, then continue with regular cleaning to maintain results.
